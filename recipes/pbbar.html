Peanut Butter Bar Recipe

An amazing treat that will satisfy any craving for peanut butter and chocolate

  • 1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, or more to taste
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1他 cups unbleached flour
  • 他 teaspoon baking soda
  • 10 ounces chocolate chunks (such as Ghirardelli速 Bittersweet Chocolate)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
  2. Beat Butter, white sugar, and brown sugar together with an electic mixer in a large bowl until light and fluffy. Beat egg and vanilla extract into the butter mixture. Add peanut butter and beat until incorporated.
  3. Sift flour and baking soda together in a seperate bowl; mix into butter mixture. Fold chocolate chunks into the mixture.
  4. Spread the batter into a 13x9-inch baking dish.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 20 to 25 minutes. Cool completely before cutting into squares.
